Job Description:

We are seeking a talented Game Graphics Designer and Illustrator to join our client’s team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating visually stunning graphics and illustrations that enhance the gaming experience. This role requires a strong artistic sense, creativity, and the ability to collaborate effectively with other team members.


Competitive starting salary of 35,000 to 45,000 per month.


Type: Remote


Working Hours: 8 hours a day, 5 days a week, 40 hrs a week(Timing Flexible)


Please send your resume and preference of expectations, flexibility, and benefits to hr@talentsathi.com, we will fast-track the application process.


Responsibilities of the Candidate:

– Create game graphics, including characters, environments, and assets.

– Make animations for game elements using Adobe Animate and Blender.

– Work with game designers to smoothly integrate visual and interactive elements.

– Use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to make game assets.

– Edit and add sound elements into games using Audacity and Adobe Audition.

– Build and improve game prototypes using Sketch and Adobe XD.

– Ensure all visual and sound elements match the game design and enhance user experience.

– Keep up with new design trends, tools, and technologies.

– Join brainstorming sessions and share creative ideas.

– Communicate clearly with team members and stakeholders to meet project requirements.


Requirements:

– 2yrs+ Experience in game design, graphic design, and illustration.

– Strong creativity and artistic skills, with a portfolio of your work.

– Skilled in animation with experience using Adobe Animate and Blender.

– Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

– Experience with sound editing software like Audacity and Adobe Audition.

– Familiar with prototyping tools such as Sketch and Adobe XD.

– Understanding of game design and ability to create engaging game graphics.

– Good communication skills and ability to work well in a team.

– Ability to handle multiple projects and meet deadlines.

– Intermediate level of English proficiency or higher.


﻿Please send your resume and preference of expectations, flexibility, and benefits to hr@talentsathi.com, we will fast-track the application process.
